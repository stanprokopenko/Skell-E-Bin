About Proko

Proko is an online art school that offers courses on a wide range of topics. It provides a respected and fun learning environment for artists of all skill levels.

Course Topics

Proko's courses cover the following art topics:

Drawing

  • Learn fundamental drawing skills and techniques to improve your art.
  • Courses cover topics such as perspective, shading, and composition.

Painting

  • Discover various painting techniques and mediums.
  • Learn how to mix colors, create textures, and develop your own painting style.

Sculpting

  • Explore the world of sculpting with courses on different techniques and materials.
  • Learn how to create 3D forms and bring your ideas to life.

Anatomy

  • Gain a deep understanding of human anatomy for artists.
  • Learn how to accurately depict the human form in your artwork.

Concepting

  • Develop your skills in concept art and design.
  • Learn how to create compelling characters, environments, and props.

Comics

  • Dive into the world of comic art and storytelling.
  • Learn how to create engaging comics from start to finish.

Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the fundamentals or an experienced artist seeking to refine your skills, Proko has a course for you. With a wide range of topics and a fun, engaging learning environment, Proko is your go-to online art school for all your artistic needs.
